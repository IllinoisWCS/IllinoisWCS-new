About Explorations

A warm welcome to the WCS Explorations, WCS’s sub-committee to help you with all things technical! We’re here to help you with everything ranging from technical skill development to side projects to interview prep to hackathons with the final goal of presenting an exciting project at UIUC’s annual Engineering Open House. We’ll be having a range of tutorials that would give you an introduction to a new tool/technology, useful tips and tricks and resources for further development! We’ll also have monthly socials to make sure you can meet some awesome people you can potentially work with in the future. You can join our Facebook group to stay up to date with our events. We look forward to meeting you and having a great semester! If you have any questions or suggestions, feel free to email us - Mihika Aggarwal or Alice Fang.
